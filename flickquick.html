FlickQuick

Team: Ashwath Anand, Utsav Parasrampuria
Duration: 8 months.
My role: User Experience design, Visual Design and Branding


Challenge

With FlickQuick we set out to change the way people share photos, especially at big events. FlickQuick is our take on a seamless, intuitive and convenient way to share pictures with friends so everyone can have more fun and make sure they dont loose any precious memories.

Process

The first stage involved a map of features that would be required. This was followed by a few rounds of paper prototyping and a lot of sketching.


The second stage involved making interactive wireframes on indesign and user testing them with a focus group in India.




The third stage involved branding work and adding visual design to the wireframes. The following are some screens from the visual design phase.


These have been sent to a developer team and we are developing a beta version now for further testing and getting it ready for the final launch.


Finally, We made a website that gives interested users some vital information, for event managers to partner up with FlickQuick and a way to subscribe and be up to date as FlickQuick grows.
