Project details

I worked as a technical project manager on Junior's embedded project with Shyp. We built their marketing website: shyp.com and their new estimator.

As technical program manager, I was part of the decisionmaking team that made decisions on deadlines and deliverables. I owned the scrum process from doing standups, delegating tasks and coordinated with Junior's design team, Junior's tech team and Shyp's tech team. I also owned the QA process, done mostly through bugherd.

The development framework I setup for Shyp, was very helpful to finish the project high quality and on time. I also had significant UX design contributions to Shyp's new estimator tool.
