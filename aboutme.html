Designer • Engineer • Mad Scientist

Hi ! My name is Ashwath.

I am a Designer and I am attracted to simple products that work well, Products that do complex things and are easy to interact with. Design should make life enjoyable and simple and this is what I look for through my design work. I love facing challenges and passionately solve them.

My undergraduate degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering gives me a multidisciplinary approach to all the projects I do. My projects cover Interaction Design, Service Design and User Experience Design, I feel this lets me be an Engineer and Designer.

I feel technology is a perfect blend of Design and Engineering and the best products we use are a testament to this. Making products, apps and services that facilitate the better use of technology is where my future is.

I am currently pursuing a Master of Fine Arts degree in Industrial Design from Savannah College of Art and Design and am always on the lookout for opportunities to collaborate on.
