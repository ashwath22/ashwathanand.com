Oh hello there

My name is Ashwath. Most people like to call me Ash. I am a multi disciplinary problem solver and I love making products and services that solve problems in a clever way and are easy and joyful to interact with. I guess thats a fancy way of saying i love making cool things.

I have an MFA in Industrial design and a Bachelors degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. Since June 2015 I have been working at Junior in San Francisco as a creative technologist and technical program manager. At junior I have been lucky enough to work on some very cool projects like a webVR app where anyone can create interactive VR experiences, a very cocky and talkative twitterbot, an AI driven chatbot and many more.
