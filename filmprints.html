Filmprints

Duration: October 2016 - current
Tech stack: Contentful CMS, node.js, express, handlebars.js and heroku

Film prints has been my opportunity to comine two of my current obsessions: building full-stack javascript web apps and learning to shoot on a 30 year old film camera. This is still a work in progress and will continue to get better as I get better at product design, web development and photography.
