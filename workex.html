Work Experience

My work experience from my two internships, one freelance project and developing this portfolio website from scratch has given me a lot of exposure to the user experince design process. I focus on information architecture, producing wireframes, user testing and iterative development upto visual design phase.
As a designer I strive to come up with creative solutions. My knowledge of Engineering and code means I have empathy for the Engineers who develop products. I have progressively grown with each project I have taken up and I strongly believe I am a good bridge between creativity, engineering and the end user.

All the work described here cannot be published. But I can share some of it in person.

Leaf Holdings

(Click on left and right of all images on this page to scroll through them.)

I worked as a User Experience Design Intern at Leaf in the Summer of 2014.
Some of the work I did at Leaf:
  • Improved features on point of sale(POS) device like delete, split bill, applying discounts, and many more.
  • Worked on improving usability of Leaf Business, the content manager for merchants enrolled with Leaf POS.
  • Worked on two ios apps as part of an MVP for a new service Leaf is trying to create using Beacons in a retail setting. I created information architecture, wireframes and visual designs for the two apps.
  • Improved Leaf's user testing Lab by setting up live stream video recording.
  • Created high fidelity interactive wireframes for four user testing sessions.


    • Idea Spice LLC


    I worked as a User Experience Design Intern at Idea Spice LLC in Dubai from December 2013 to February 2014.
    Some of the work I did at Idea Spice:
  • Developed information architecture, wireframes and basic visual design for two mobile applications and one web application.
  • The first mobile app was a lunch ordering app. The existing ones were difficult to use and were very dated design wise so Idea Spice wanted to capitalize on the huge opportunity in that space though this app.
  • The Second mobile app was for business executives to get updates about the company's daily numbers and performance through simple infographics.
  • The web application was to consume multimedia content according to the user's mood.
