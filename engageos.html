EngageOS

Duration: December 2016- January 2017 Team: Ashwath Anand, Jordan Lawler, Robbie whiting, J.P.Zundel My role: creative technologist, research facilitator Tech stack: Messenger platform, Watson knowledge studio, node.js, express

Project details

I am not allowed to disclose details from this project yet as it is still under development.

That being said, this is a project to buile a messenger based, AI driven chatbot concierge for delegates at a conference.
I have seen this project from its inception to its current state. It started off with a workshop with the client to understand the deeper problems and insights. I facilitated that workshop.

After a synthesis of the insights from the workshop we were able to draft up a feature set for the product.
For the feature presentation I built chatbot flows on FAcebook's messenger platform to prototype what the manifestation of those features would look like.

I am gald to show the messenger chatbot flows in person on request.
