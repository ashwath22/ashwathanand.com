Domed



Team: Ashwath, Jonathan, Yuan and Richard
Duration: 1 week.
My role: Writing arduino code, circuit building and installation of Domed in the space.
Softwares: Arduino, Illustrator.



The Challenge

This project started as an assignment to make a fun interactive installation with the contents of a 'magic' cup given to us in class.
Our cup contained an infrared motion sensor, a compass, a small speaker and a few LEDs.



The Idea

After some brain storming we decided to build an interactive music device using the components.We envisioned this to be an installment in a place where busy people could stop for a few seconds and get some laughs with just their cell phones.







Inside the Dome

Construction

After a lot of testing and iteration, we were able to build the circuit and also built the housing for it using lego bricks.
