GS1 Telepresence Robot

GS1 is a telepresence robot service. With just a weblink and facetime, anyone from anywhere in the world will be able to take part in the campus tours at SCAD.

VendoBot

With VendoBot we set out to create a platform that will serve as a physical touchpoint for students to experiment with as well as a representation of the nature of projects being carried out by students of Interaction Design at SCAD's School of Design.


See more about VendoBot

Domed

Domed started as an assignment to make a fun interactive installation with the contents of a 'magic' cup given to us in class.
Our cup contained an infrared motion sensor, a compass, a small speaker and a few LEDs.
We created an interactive music installation with the components we were given.
